About DH Forge

Small free browser tools for everyday work

Free Toolbox is a static website for quick utilities such as character counts, date calculations, unit conversion, QR codes, printable paper, and practice sheets.

What Matters Here

  • No account, payment, or comment system is required to use the core tools.
  • Text, images, and template settings are designed to be handled in the current browser tab when possible.
  • The tools stay focused on quick calculators, converters, printable pages, and simple worksheet tasks.

Site Sections

Free Toolbox focuses on calculators and converters. Printable Paper Lab focuses on printable templates. Kids Practice Lab focuses on practice sheets for home and class.
