Privacy Policy

Free Toolbox is a static website that does not provide account registration, login, comments, payments, or server file uploads. Most tool input is processed in the user's browser.

Information We Do Not Request

  • We do not ask for sensitive personal information such as legal IDs or phone numbers.
  • We do not create or store member account information.
  • Text, QR, and image tools are designed to process input in the browser rather than saving original input to our own server.

Browser-Side Processing

Character counts, date calculations, unit conversion, random picking, QR generation, color conversion, timers, ratios, and image resizing run with JavaScript in the browser. This site does not use its own database for those tools.

Advertising and Cookies

Advertising services such as Google AdSense may be added later. If ads are enabled, Google and advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, browser information, and similar identifiers for ad delivery, measurement, personalization, and abuse prevention.

Google explains partner-site data use at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

External Services

This site may use Google Analytics to understand visits, page views, and tool-use events. Google Analytics may process cookies, IP addresses, browser and device information, visited pages, and event data for site measurement, improvement, and troubleshooting.

The base features are designed without external fonts or image CDNs. If advertising or additional analytics tools are added, those providers' policies may also apply.
