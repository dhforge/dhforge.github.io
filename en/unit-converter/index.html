Unit Converter

Convert common length, weight, temperature, and area units in one place.

How to use this unit converter

Select a category, enter a number, and choose the current unit. The tool converts the value into all supported units in the same category, which is useful for product sizes, recipes, rooms, temperatures, and quick comparisons.

Length

Convert mm, cm, m, km, inches, and feet.

Weight and temperature

Convert grams, kilograms, pounds, ounces, Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin.

Area

Compare square meters, pyeong, square feet, and acres.

Steps

  1. Choose the conversion type.
  2. Enter the value and source unit.
  3. Read the converted values in the result list.

Examples

  • Converting centimeters to inches for product dimensions
  • Checking square meters and pyeong together
  • Switching between Celsius and Fahrenheit
Are results rounded?

Results are trimmed for readability. Use a specialist tool for scientific precision.

What is the pyeong conversion?

One pyeong is calculated as 3.305785 square meters.

Are values stored?

No. The conversion runs in your current browser tab.
