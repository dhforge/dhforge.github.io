How to use this character counter

Paste text into the box and the page instantly calculates characters with spaces, characters without spaces, UTF-8 bytes, words, lines, and estimated reading time. It is useful for bios, titles, descriptions, applications, and short writing limits.

Characters with spaces

Counts every visible character plus spaces and line breaks.

Characters without spaces

Removes whitespace before counting the remaining characters.

UTF-8 bytes

Shows byte length for text that may include Korean, English, symbols, or numbers.

Steps

  1. Paste the text you want to check.
  2. Review the character, byte, word, and line results.
  3. Edit the text until it fits your limit.

Examples

  • Checking a social profile length
  • Adjusting a blog title or meta description
  • Estimating the length of a short announcement
Is my text saved?

No. The calculation runs in your browser tab and the text is not sent to this site's own server.

Why can byte count be different from character count?

UTF-8 can use multiple bytes for one character, especially for non-Latin text and symbols.

What does without spaces remove?

It removes spaces, tabs, and line breaks before counting.
