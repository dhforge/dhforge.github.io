Privacy Policy

Printable Paper Lab is a static website that generates printable templates in the browser. It does not provide account registration, login, comments, payment, or server-side template storage.

Information We Do Not Request

  • We do not ask for sensitive personal information.
  • We do not create user accounts or store template settings in our own database.
  • Template titles and settings are processed in the current browser tab.

Advertising and Cookies

Advertising services such as Google AdSense may be added later. If ads are enabled, Google and advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, browser information, and similar identifiers for ad delivery, measurement, personalization, and abuse prevention.

Google explains partner-site data use at How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

External Services

This site may use Google Analytics to understand visits, page views, and template-use events. Google Analytics may process cookies, IP addresses, browser and device information, visited pages, and event data for site measurement, improvement, and troubleshooting.

The base features are designed without external fonts, external images, or user-upload servers. If advertising or additional analytics tools are added later, those services' policies may also apply.
