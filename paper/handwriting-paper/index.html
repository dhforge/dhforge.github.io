Handwriting Paper Generator

Create handwriting practice paper with baseline and midline guides for letters, spacing, and neat writing exercises.

How to use handwriting paper

Use the stronger line as the baseline and the lighter guide as a midline. Larger spacing works well for early practice, while smaller spacing is better for older students or compact drills.

Letter practice

Print pages for repeated letters, names, words, or short sentences.

Spacing guide

The midline helps keep letter height consistent.

Flexible title

Add a lesson name, date, student name, or leave the page blank.

Is this for a specific curriculum?

No. It is a general blank practice page.

Can adults use it?

Yes. It can be used for any handwriting practice or penmanship drills.

Does the site store student names?

No. Any title text is only rendered in the browser tab.
