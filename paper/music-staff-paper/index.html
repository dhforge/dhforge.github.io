Printable Music Paper

Music Staff Paper

Generate blank staff paper for composing, notation practice, melody sketches, music lessons, and rehearsal notes.

How to use music staff paper

Print a blank page and write clefs, notes, chords, rhythm exercises, or short melody ideas by hand. The page is intentionally generic, so it does not require ongoing updates.

Composition

Sketch ideas before moving them into notation software.

Practice

Use staff lines for theory exercises, intervals, scales, and rhythm drills.

Lessons

Print quick pages for music class or private lesson notes.

Does it include clefs?

No. It is blank staff paper, so you can write any clef or notation style you need.

Can I save it as PDF?

Yes. Click Print or save PDF and select Save as PDF in your browser.

Can I use it for commercial music?

The blank page can be printed, but you are responsible for the rights to any music you write on it.
