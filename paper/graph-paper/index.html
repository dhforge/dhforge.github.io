Printable Grid Paper

Graph Paper Generator

Create square grid paper for math, drawing, charts, design sketches, and planning. Adjust the paper size, spacing, line color, and title before printing.

How to use the graph paper generator

Choose Letter or A4, pick the grid spacing, and use the print button. In the print dialog, select your printer or choose Save as PDF to keep a reusable file.

Math practice

Use square grids for arithmetic, geometry, coordinate work, and classroom worksheets.

Sketching

Plan layouts, icons, furniture, or small diagrams with evenly spaced grid lines.

PDF workflow

Browser printing lets you save the generated page as a PDF without extra software.

Can I change the final print scale?

Yes. Use your browser or printer scale setting if you need exact physical spacing.

Does the page store my title?

No. The title is only used in the current browser tab.

Can I use this for classroom handouts?

Yes. You may print templates for ordinary classroom and personal use.
