Printable Study Template

Cornell Notes Template

Generate a Cornell notes page with a cue column, notes area, and summary box. Useful for lectures, reading notes, and study review.

How to use Cornell notes

Write key questions or cues on the left, detailed notes on the right, and a short summary at the bottom. The format is simple and does not depend on a specific school year or curriculum.

Cue column

Use the left section for keywords, questions, vocabulary, or review prompts.

Notes area

Use the right section for main notes, examples, diagrams, and references.

Summary box

Write the most important points after class or after reading.

Is this tied to a specific class?

No. It is a blank study template and can be used for any subject.

Can I remove the title?

Yes. Leave the title field empty for a blank page.

Can I save it as PDF?

Yes. Use Print or save PDF and choose Save as PDF in the browser dialog.
