Printable Paper Lab FAQ

Do these templates cost anything?

No. The paper tools are free to use in your browser.

Can I save a template as PDF?

Yes. Click Print and choose Save as PDF in your browser's print dialog.

Are my titles or settings saved?

No. The tool does not create accounts or store template settings on this site's own server.

Which paper sizes are supported?

The generator supports Letter and A4 preview ratios. Your final print size is controlled by the browser print dialog and printer settings.
